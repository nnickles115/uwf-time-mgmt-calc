How to use this calculator:

  1. Enter the number of credits you are taking. The calculator will automatically calculate the number of hours reccomended to study. Ideally, for every hour you are in class, 2-3 hours should be spent outside of class studying. This number is adjustable if you believe you require less studying.
  2. Then enter the number of hours spent on other activities. By default, it is assumed that you are getting 8 hours of sleep per night. Whether the amount is more or less, having a consistent schedule is very helpful.
  3. Based on your results, adjust your time as needed to help better balance your work/life schedule.
