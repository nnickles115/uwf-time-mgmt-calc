How to use this calculator:

  • Enter the number of credits you are taking. The calculator will automatically calculate the number of hours recommended to study. Ideally, for every hour you are in class, 2-3 hours should be spent outside of class studying. This number is adjustable if you believe you require less studying.
  • Enter the number of hours spent on other activities. By default, it is assumed that you are getting 8 hours of sleep per night. Whether the amount is more or less, having a consistent schedule is very helpful.
  • Based on your results, adjust your time as needed to help better balance your work/life schedule.
